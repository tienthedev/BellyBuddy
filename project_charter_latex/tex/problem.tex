Patients with Gastrointestinal (GI) diseases such as Ulcerative Colitis or Crohn's Disease are needed to record a lot of detailed information about their diet and health to understand their triggers and manage their health. This is a tedious task with current health apps that focus on separate solutions. By consolidating patient's health into one place, making it easier to track your food sensitivities, identifying triggers, and allowing effective sharing with physicians, would utmost help with their health decisions more easily.
